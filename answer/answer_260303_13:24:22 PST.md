## Notes

- **Azure AI Foundry Hub and AML Workspace** both require `Contributor` at the resource group level because they provision multiple linked resources (Storage, Key Vault, ACR, App Insights) at creation time — no narrower built-in role covers all of these.
- **Azure AI Foundry Project creation** can be delegated to `Azure AI Developer` on the Hub, avoiding `Contributor` for most team members.
- **Azure OpenAI model deployment** is a separate operation from resource creation — use `Cognitive Services OpenAI Contributor` scoped to the OpenAI resource for the team that manages deployments.
- **Azure AI Search index creation** (data plane) requires `Search Index Data Contributor` on the Search Service — distinct from `Search Service Contributor` which creates the service itself.
- **Bot Service** has no purpose-built role — `Contributor` is the minimum for all operations.
- **Cross-resource roles** above should be assigned to the **managed identity** of each resource, not to human users, following least-privilege principles.
